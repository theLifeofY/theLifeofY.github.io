Wedding Envelopes

We have been happily living together in our 700 sq ft apartment in Corvallis, OR for several years now, and therefore have accumulated all the things we might need, so we ask for your presence not presents! If you’d still like to celebrate us with a gift, we’re choosing to follow the Chinese and Korean gifting tradition of “hongbao” or “chug ui-geum” or lucky money, which is provided to the newlyweds day of to bless their wedding day and married life. You can also contribute to some of our online funds and help support future goals and specific social justice initiatives we are passionate about.
