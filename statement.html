Divesting from Big Tech in the Wedding Industry

In an effort to divest from Big Tech (Google, Facebook, Netflix, Microsoft, etc) and protect the privacy of our event and guests' information, we are taking the following measures to eliminate the possibility of your data being sold to third-parties. Choosing Queer and Vendors of Color

As a queer and interracial couple, we want to support queer and POC vendors. The harms of the wedding industry in America are often obscured, from violations of one's data and privacy to to the whitewashing of wedding culture despite POC comprising more than 40% of Americans. If you're interested in the vendors we chose and why, please reach out to us!
