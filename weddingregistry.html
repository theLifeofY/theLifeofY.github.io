Our Wedding Registry

Ways to Contribute

Wedding Red Envelopes

First off, we are so thankful that you could join us to celebrate our wedding, and any additional gift on top of your presence is not necessary. However, should you wish to contribute to our future home and travel funds, we would love for you to use the red wedding envelope that you have received along with your invitation.

Your red wedding envelope follows the Chinese and Korean gifting traditions of "hongbao," "chug ui-geum," or "lucky money." This money is provided to newlyweds on the day of their wedding to bless the start of their married life together. Additionally, we ask everyone, regardless of a monetary gift, to please bring your red envelope filled with the gift of a word of encouragement or a word of advice that you have for us as we begin this next chapter of our lives together.

Because we anticipate a move in the near future as we continue to establish our careers and a more permanent home together, we kindly request that no physical items be gifted to us during this time.
